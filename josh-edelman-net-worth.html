FAQs About Josh Edelman

Who is Josh Edelman?

Josh Edelman is a multifaceted entertainer known for his work as a comedian, filmmaker, podcast host, and magician. He has been acknowledged for his contributions to comedy and filmmaking, notably through his stand-up performances and his documentary work.

What achievements does he have in stand-up comedy?

As a stand-up comedian, he has been a regular at the Hollywood Improv, ranked among the top 50 Roast Battlers at the Comedy Store, won the #UAreFunny stand-up competition three times, and headlined the Electric Comedy Festival 2021.

He was also recognized as one of the Top 100 Comics to Watch in LA.

What films has he directed?

He directed Mentally Al, a stand-up comedy documentary acclaimed as the Best Comedy Documentary 2021. He’s also the creator and star of Best Friends! Web Series, which triumphed at the Oktoberfest Film Festival in NYC.

What is the LA Comedy Underground?

He is the creator of the LA Comedy Underground. This venture produces three of LA’s most popular monthly shows.

The shows are Well Dressed at The Hollywood Roosevelt, Undiscovered Genius at the Hollywood Improv, and Gripe Juice at Bar Covell. Remarkably, each of these shows consistently achieves sell-out status every month.

Has he received any recognition as a magician?

Before his comedy career, he was a professional magician, highlighted in Time Out New York and on BET’s The Pull Up.

He’s performed for celebrities including The New York Yankees, Dan Marino, and Steve Buscemi. Occasionally, he showcases his comedy magic routine.

Where does he perform?

He performs nationwide, including prestigious venues like Carolines in NYC, The Laugh Factory in Chicago, and The Dania Beach Improv. In Los Angeles, he’s a regular at The Hollywood Improv and The Hollywood Roosevelt Hotel.
