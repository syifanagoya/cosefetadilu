Ariana Grande Posts Some Adorable Pics And Vids From Her And New Hubby Dalton Gomezs Honeymoon!

March 2024 · 2 minute read

After previously tying the knot with Dalton Gomez in a secret, intimate wedding and then later sharing some gorgeous pics from the ceremony, Ariana Grande is now letting her fans in when it comes to their honeymoon as well! The singer posted several pictures and even footage from their honeymoon in the Netherlands!

Earlier today, Ari took to her IG account to share the images with her millions of followers.

The happy newlyweds chose to spend their honeymoon in Amsterdam following their surprise wedding in May.

To signify that they were there, Ariana captioned her post with snail and waffle emojis since both are associated with the capital of The Netherlands.

One of the pictures shows the happy couple chilling in a pair of massive wooden shoes, located all throughout the region precisely so that tourists can take wacky pictures with them!

But that was not all as the talented songstress shared more pics from the trip, a second being of a windmill and another of one of Amsterdam’s well known canals!

On the same day, Ariana also shared a video on her IG Stories, which was apparently taken at a jazz club and another featuring some cows in a field, which seemed to be captured while the couple was on a train, going by them!
