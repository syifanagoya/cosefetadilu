FAQs About Angela Kinsey

Who is Angela Kinsey?

She is an American actress best known for her role as Angela Martin on the hit TV show The Office. She has also appeared in other TV shows and movies like Haters Back Off! and Your Family or Mine.

What is her background?

She was born on June 25, 1971, in Lafayette, Louisiana. She spent part of her childhood in Jakarta, Indonesia, before moving back to the U.S. and eventually settling in Texas. She attended Baylor University, where she graduated with a degree in English.

What did she do before acting?

Before landing her role on The Office, she worked various jobs, including as an operator for 1-800-DENTIST and doing behind-the-scenes work on Late Night with Conan O’Brien. She also performed improv at the Groundlings Theatre in Los Angeles.

How did she get her role on The Office?

She initially auditioned for the role of Pam Beesly but didn’t get it. However, the casting directors felt she was perfect for the role of Angela Martin, the stern head of accounting. Her performance made her a standout character on the show.

Is she like her character on The Office?
